SMVITM

Founded in 2010 by H.H. Shri Vishwavallabha Theertha Swamiji of Shri Sode Vadiraj Mutt, Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management,situated at Bantakal in Udupi has carved a niche for itself in imparting quality engineering education in the coastal Karnataka region.

COURSES
  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

WHY SMVITM

  • To create a transparent and attractive academic environment that encourages pursuit.
  • To inculculate in the students and the teachers, a scientific temper
  • To develop and nurture a value-based but thoroughly professional educational system
